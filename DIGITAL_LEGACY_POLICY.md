# Wasi Digital Legacy Policy

This app must not promise full control over every public internet record. The professional and compliant product scope is:

1. The user records digital accounts and legacy instructions.
2. The app stores instructions for each platform account.
3. The app may help guardians prepare official closure, memorialization, archive, or transfer requests.
4. Final execution depends on each platform's official policy, API permissions, identity checks, and legal proof.
5. No automatic action should run until the death-verification workflow is complete.

## Required verification flow

- User inactivity threshold is reached.
- Guardians are notified.
- At least one authorized guardian confirms.
- Legal proof is uploaded or recorded.
- A manual review step is completed before any external request is generated.

## Product wording

Use: digital legacy management, account instructions, guardian workflow, official request assistant.

Do not use: delete all internet data, access all social accounts, remove every public record automatically.
